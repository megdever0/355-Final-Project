Frequently Asked Questions

What is the sign up fee for the league?

The sign up fee is $50 for the season.

What does the sign up fee include?

The sign up fee includes access to the fields and the uniform shirt for the team you will play on.

Which team should I join?

You can join any team you like based on availability. Contact the league director Megan Devereux for more information.

What happens if I miss a practice or game?

There is no penalty for missing any practices or games.
