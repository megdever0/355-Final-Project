About Our League

The Queens Intramural Softball League is a coed softball league based in New York City's best borough, Queens.

This league was founded in 2020 by Megan Devereux.

Anyone 18 and older can play.

Our softball season last throughout the month of May.

We have 4 teams:

The Lions

The Blue Jays

The Dolphins

The Pythons

If you are looking for a competitive and inclusive softball league, look no further

The Queens Intramural Softball League is the right place for YOU!

I take a lot of pride running the Queens Intramural Softball League. I do my best to make all the players and staff feel welcomed and have the opportunity to play this great sport.

Megan Devereux

Queens Intramural Softball League Manager
